About

 

Immersion App

A desktop application for spaced repetition flashcard study, immersion material tracking (Comming Soon), and review session management.

Version 1.0.0

 

Features

  • Spaced repetition system (SRS) with customizable scheduling
  • Anki deck import (.apkg)
  • Custom card types and field templates
  • Review statistics and activity heatmap
  • Immersion time tracking(Coming Soon)
